Catechization

Family, the cell that makes the society, needs to live up to her expectations of being a domestic church, where formation begins. Aside from being the nursery for worthy Christians, it is also a unit that should level up on faith and knowledge.

With this in the background, the Knight of St. John International, in Lagos Grand, has put together a family catechesis. The program is aimed at encouraging families to study the scriptures and important text of the church. It also promotes unity and growth among all members of the family unit.
